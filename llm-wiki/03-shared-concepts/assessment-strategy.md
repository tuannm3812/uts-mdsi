# Assessment Strategy

## Definition

A repeatable process for turning assessment briefs and rubrics into plans, drafts, checks, and final submissions.

## Workflow

1. Extract task requirements.
2. Convert rubric criteria into a checklist.
3. Draft against the checklist.
4. Ask the LLM for a strict critique.
5. Verify evidence, citations, code, and calculations.
