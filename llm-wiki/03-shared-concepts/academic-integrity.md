# Academic Integrity

## Definition

The rules and practices that keep coursework honest, attributable, and verifiable.

## Why It Matters

LLMs can help with explanation, critique, drafting structure, and study, but final submitted work must remain your own and must follow UTS subject rules.

## Connected Subjects

- All subjects

## LLM Use Checklist

- Keep prompts and outputs when they materially shaped an assessment.
- Verify citations and factual claims.
- Do not submit generated text blindly.
- Follow the assessment-specific AI policy.
