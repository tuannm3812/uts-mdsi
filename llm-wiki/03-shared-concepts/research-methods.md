# Research Methods

## Definition

Methods for asking defensible questions, gathering evidence, evaluating claims, and reporting limitations.

## Review Questions

- What claim is being made?
- What evidence supports it?
- What assumptions are required?
- What are the limitations?
- What would change the conclusion?
